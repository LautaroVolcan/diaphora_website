About

Let me introduce myself.

I am a trained DJ, singer and multi-instrumentalist, and my sets combine technical precision with creativity and groove. I move seamlessly between electronic music and timeless classics, exploring Tech House, Funky House, Techno, and Nu Disco, while also performing Disco, Pop, Latin, R&B, Country, Jazz, Rap, Ballads, and Rock Classics. On stage, I bring a strong presence and the ability to connect authentically with the audience, always adapting to the energy of the room. I am confident with microphone use, enjoy engaging directly with people, and maintain professionalism and commitment in every performance. With a background in music education, years of international experience, and perfect English fluency, I combine versatility, reliability, and creativity to deliver an immersive and memorable musical journey across genres and eras.
